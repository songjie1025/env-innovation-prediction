## One-Paragraph Summary

RISE documents regulatory indicators for sustainable energy, including policy and regulatory conditions related to energy access, renewable energy, and energy efficiency. For this project, RISE is a possible alternative or supplement to OECD EPS, especially if the analysis needs a sustainable-energy policy measure beyond OECD coverage. Its relevance is currently weaker than EPS because the project has not downloaded or checked RISE coverage, and the literature evidence already identified is more directly connected to OECD-style environmental policy and patent outcomes. RISE may become useful if its country-year coverage aligns well with the target and if the project wants a specific energy-policy predictor. Until then, it should stay optional.

### 一段式摘要

RISE 记录 sustainable energy 的监管指标，包括与 energy access、renewable energy 和 energy efficiency 相关的政策与监管条件。对本项目而言，RISE 是 OECD EPS 的一个可能替代项或补充项，尤其是在分析需要 OECD 覆盖范围之外的 sustainable-energy policy measure 时。它目前的相关性弱于 EPS，因为项目尚未下载或检查 RISE 覆盖范围，而且已经识别的文献证据与 OECD-style environmental policy 和 patent outcomes 的联系更直接。如果 RISE 的 country-year 覆盖与目标变量很好对齐，并且项目需要一个具体的 energy-policy predictor，它可能变得有用。在此之前，它应保持为 optional。
