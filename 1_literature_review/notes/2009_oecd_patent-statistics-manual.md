## One-Paragraph Summary

The OECD Patent Statistics Manual is a general methodological reference for constructing, interpreting, and comparing patent indicators. It is not specific to environmental innovation, but it is important for explaining patent-counting choices, country attribution, time attribution, fractional counting, patent families, normalization, and interpretation limits. For this project, it strengthens the measurement section by giving a broader patent-statistics foundation behind the OECD environmental patent target. It also helps justify why the report should avoid treating patent indicators as complete measures of innovation or environmental performance. The manual is especially useful for anticipating reviewer concerns about cross-country comparability and patent indicator normalization.

### 一段式摘要

`OECD Patent Statistics Manual` 是关于构造、解释和比较 patent indicators 的通用方法参考。它并不专门针对 environmental innovation，但对解释 patent-counting choices、country attribution、time attribution、fractional counting、patent families、normalization 和 interpretation limits 很重要。对本项目而言，它通过提供 OECD environmental patent target 背后更广义的 patent-statistics 基础，加强了测量部分。它也有助于说明为什么报告应避免把 patent indicators 当作 innovation 或 environmental performance 的完整度量。该手册尤其适合用来预先回应评审对 cross-country comparability 和 patent indicator normalization 的疑问。
